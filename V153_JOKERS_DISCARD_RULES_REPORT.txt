Baseline
- Built from rummy500-v152-vercel-install-fix.zip.
- Kept the V152 Vercel install fix, Node 24 setting, saved games, shared rooms, phone-seat mode, and playable-cardgame foundation.

Added
- Deck is now 56 cards: the normal 52-card deck plus 4 jokers.
- Jokers are worth 15 points.
- Aces are worth 15 points.
- 2–9 are worth 5 points.
- 10, J, Q, and K are worth 10 points.
- Jokers can be used inside sets and runs.
- When a joker is used, the meld display shows what card the joker represents, for example 🃏=7♣.
- Players can lay off a joker onto an existing meld. The app assigns the joker to the next valid card.
- Players can exchange a joker from a meld by selecting the matching real card in their hand, selecting the meld, and pressing Exchange joker.
- The exchanged joker goes back into that player's hand and can be used again.
- Players can now pick up only the top discard or the whole discard pile.
- If a player picks up the whole discard pile, the app requires them to make a new meld that turn.
- If they discard without making a meld after taking the whole discard pile, the app automatically adds a -50 penalty round for that player.
